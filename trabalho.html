Importance of the Internet in Pandemic Times

Posted by Thiago on September 28, 2021

In this pandemic, we had several problems, including financial, health and work problems, in addition to the social side.

But we had a lot of help from our technology, especially the internet, which is so vast and can be used very well.

Through it many people held meetings for jobs (which was essential for many people), religious services and even worked through it and did school lessons.



The internet can be very bad sometimes, after all, anyone can use it, however, if we use it to the best of our ability, always helping to establish the global good, we can go further and further extract the best that the internet has to offer .
